Unspool Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2025

Introduction

Unspool ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, age, and gender
  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Voice recordings and their transcriptions
  • Journal entries and associated metadata
  • Mood scores and journaling habits

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To process and store your journal entries
  • To transcribe voice recordings into text
  • To provide personalized journaling prompts
  • To send notifications and reminders
  • To analyze app usage and improve our services

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption of journal entries
  • Secure PIN protection for app access
  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore for data storage
  • Google Sign-in for authentication
  • OpenAI API for voice transcription
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging for notifications

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we may have collected information about a child under 13, please contact us.
