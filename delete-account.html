Unspool - Account Deletion

Learn how to delete your account and associated data

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

You can delete your account directly from the Unspool app:

  1. Open the Unspool app
  2. Go to the Account tab
  3. Scroll to the bottom
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your choice

Option 2: Manual Request

If you can't access your account, email us with:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Unspool"
  • Your registered email address
  • The approximate date you created your account

Send your request to: your-email@domain.com

What Gets Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • All journal entries and transcriptions
  • Personal information (name, age, gender)
  • Account settings and preferences
  • Usage data and analytics

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • All personal data is deleted immediately
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 30 days
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 7 days
